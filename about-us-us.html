What is Scriptbank

Scriptbank is a decentralized baning systems that uses Scriptbill crediting system. The meaning of decentralized is that the storage of financial information is distributed among clients instead of stored with the admin of the bank. This is against the centralized systems we often us for our fiat transaction, which raises a main security concern, especially in online trasactional security.

This means instead of creating new protocol to support financial security, like any new financial organization would do, we develop a structure that supports it, which is why we employed decentralization, as it promotes transparency, which is core to online financial security.

How Scriptbank Works

Works with Exchange Markets

Scriptbank is a Crediting System, instead of a currency system. The credit is not designed to be traded on any Exchange Market in the world. Instead Scriptbank uses a decentralized Deposit and Withdrawal system to manage her credits. This means, for a anyone to hold credit in the network, he deposits money to anyone withdrawing credit. This system allows it to be possible to use the Scriptbank Credit with any currency, products or valuables you know around the world.
