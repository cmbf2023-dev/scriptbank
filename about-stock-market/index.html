What is Scriptbank Stock Market

Scriptbank Stock Market is a market created by a Company that is publicly or privately traded in the Scriptbank credit System. Scriptbank stock market can be created by any Company when the create a Scriptbank Budget and make it public or private. Investors funds this budget to hold a stocks whose equity is based on the set agreement by the business owner.

The main purpose of the Scriptbank Stock Market is to provide a transparent playground for investors to use to make their money work for them while providing support for business activities around them.

How Stock market Works?

It's Decentralized

Information about a Stock Market is stored in every concerned transaction block. So it's transparent enough for investors to monitor the activity of a stock

Dividend Payment Mandatory

Every Stock must pay either positive or negative dividend based on the business of the day and the agreement set by the business owner concerning their stocks. The range of this pay time can also be determined by the Proprietor of the business. Dividend payment is one of the tools investors consider when trying to invest or transfer funds into a stock, so it's important.

Fast Withdrawal

Because of the Involvement of the Exchange Market, Scriptbank stocks can easily be converted to the Scriptbank Credit and back to stocks without looking for an investor to buy from you. To sell this way, you'll have to sell at the market rate, not at your own convienent rate.

Can Transferred

Scriptbank Stocks can be easily transferred from one account to another. This make it to be tradeable among Scriptbank.
