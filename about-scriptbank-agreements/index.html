What is Scriptbank Agreements

Scriptbank Agreements is a system where we bind values among two or more persons in a Scriptbank Transaction. Scriptbank Agreements is so designed so that the parties involved in a transaction can have expected outcomes in a transaction. The Scriptbank Protocol give preceedence to the operations of agreements on the network, which make your Scriptbank account financially intelliegent, can operate on their own, based on set rules in the agreement.

With Scriptbank Agreements, financial transparency and control will return to every transaction we play. All financial crime will gradually become impossible as people take advantage of Scriptbank Agreements to protect their financial lives.

How Scriptbank Agreement Works?

It's Decentralized

Scriptbank Agreement is created when there is a new transaction in the network that involve two or more persons. When created, it is always encrypted and sent to the concerned account, who decrypts and decide to accepts or decline. Once accepted, the agreement plays in the recipient account, and can't be changed because it is already distributed to witnesses.

Can't be Manipulated

Scriptbank Agreement is not designed to be changed once created. If it will be changed, the creator will create another transaction to alert the concerned party of changes to the agreement, and would only be changed once accepted by the recipient. This is because, Scriptbank Agreements are protected by cryptography, any attempts to change the agreement may forfiet the account.

Cheating Impossible.

With Scriptbank Agreements, it's not possible an agreement plays in your account without your concent. You even have the right to cancel any agreement you find unworthy in your account when you notice it. If you choose to cancel the agreement, then, values associated with the transaction will be reverted. This ensures that agreements in your account are agreement you accepted and are not forced into your account or done behind your backs.
